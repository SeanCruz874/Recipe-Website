Ratatouille

A beautiful, cozy French veggie stew packed with eggplant, zucchini, peppers, and tomatoes — colorful, healthy, and full of flavor!

Preparation:

20 minutes

Cook:

40 minutes

Total:

About 1 hour

Ingredients:

  • 1 small eggplant (aubergine), diced
  • 1 zucchini (courgette), diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, diced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 ripe tomatoes, chopped (or 1 can diced tomatoes)
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh thyme (or Italian seasoning)
  • Fresh basil for garnish (optional but *chef’s kiss*)

Instructions:

  1. Heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil in a big skillet or pot. Add the eggplant and cook until slightly softened. Remove and set aside.
  2. In the same pot, add another tablespoon of olive oil. Cook the zucchini until slightly golden. Remove and set aside with the eggplant.
  3. Add the last tablespoon of olive oil. Sauté the onion and garlic until soft and fragrant.
  4. Add the diced bell peppers and cook for a few minutes until slightly tender.
  5. Add the chopped tomatoes, salt, pepper, and thyme. Let it simmer for about 5 minutes to get saucy and delicious.
  6. Return the eggplant and zucchini to the pot. Gently stir everything together.
  7. Cover and simmer on low heat for about 20-30 minutes, stirring occasionally, until all the veggies are soft and cozy together.
  8. Garnish with fresh basil and serve hot or at room temp (both ways are a vibe!).
