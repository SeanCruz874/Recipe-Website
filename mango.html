Mango Sticky Rice

Sweet Thai dessert made of sticky rice, fresh mango slices, and creamy coconut sauce — a refreshing tropical treat!

Preparation:

10 minutes

Cook:

20 minutes

Total:

30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup glutinous (sticky) rice
  • 1 ½ cups coconut milk
  • ½ cup sugar
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 2 ripe mangoes, peeled and sliced
  • 1 tablespoon toasted sesame seeds (optional)

Instructions

  1. Rinse the sticky rice several times until the water runs clear, then soak it for at least 1 hour (or overnight).
  2. Steam the rice for 20-25 minutes until tender.
  3. Meanwhile, heat the coconut milk with sugar and salt over low heat until dissolved (do not boil).
  4. Reserve ⅓ of the coconut milk mixture for topping.
  5. Mix the remaining coconut milk with the steamed rice; let it sit for 10 minutes to absorb.
  6. Serve the sticky rice with fresh mango slices and drizzle with the reserved coconut milk.
  7. Sprinkle with sesame seeds if desired and enjoy!
