Butter Chicken

Rich, creamy, and full of warm spices â€” Butter Chicken is a comforting dish that feels special but is simple enough for a cozy night in.

Preparation:

20-30 minutes active prep

Marinate Time:

1 hour (or overnight for deeper flavor)

Cook:

30 minutes

Total:

About 2 hours (mostly hands-off)

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 pounds (680g) boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1/2 cup (120g) plain yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 2 teaspoons ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • Salt, to taste
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil or butter
  • 3 tablespoons butter (for the sauce)
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon grated fresh ginger
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 can (14 oz or 400g) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 cup (240ml) heavy cream
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, mix the yogurt, lemon juice, cumin, coriander, turmeric, garam masala, chili powder, and a good pinch of salt. Add the chicken and coat well. Cover and marinate for at least 1 hour, or overnight in the fridge.
  2. Heat the oil or butter in a large skillet or pan over medium-high heat. Add the marinated chicken pieces and cook until browned on all sides. They do not have to be fully cooked through yet. Remove and set aside.
  3. In the same pan, melt the butter for the sauce. Add the chopped onion and cook until soft and golden, about 5 minutes.
  4. Stir in the garlic, ginger, cumin, paprika, and garam masala. Cook for another minute until fragrant.
  5. Add the crushed tomatoes and simmer for about 10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until slightly thickened.
  6. Return the chicken and any juices to the pan. Stir in the cream and simmer for another 10 minutes, until the chicken is cooked through and the sauce is rich and creamy.
  7. Adjust seasoning with salt if needed.
  8. Serve hot with rice or naan. Garnish with fresh cilantro if you like.
