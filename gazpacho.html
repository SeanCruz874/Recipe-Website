Gazpacho

Gazpacho is a refreshing cold soup from Spain, made with ripe tomatoes, cucumbers, peppers, and olive oil. It’s perfect for hot summer days — light, flavorful, and healthy.

Preparation:

15 minutes

Chill:

2 hours (recommended)

Total:

15 minutes (+ chilling time)

Ingredients:

  • 6 ripe tomatoes, cored and chopped
  • 1 cucumber, peeled and chopped
  • 1 small red bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 small green bell pepper, chopped
  • ½ small red onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • ½ cup cold water (adjust for desired consistency)
  • Bread cubes (optional, for thickening)

Instructions:

  1. In a blender or food processor, combine tomatoes, cucumber, bell peppers, onion, and garlic. Blend until smooth.
  2. Add red wine vinegar, olive oil, salt, and pepper. Blend again to incorporate.
  3. Add water to adjust thickness. If desired, blend in a small piece of bread to thicken further.
  4. Chill the soup in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours before serving.
  5. Stir well before serving. Serve cold, garnished with diced vegetables, croutons, or a drizzle of olive oil.
