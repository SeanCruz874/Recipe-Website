Blini

Traditional Russian pancakes â€” soft, light, and perfect for sweet or savory toppings!

Preparation:

15 minutes

Cook:

20 minutes

Total:

35 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup milk, warm
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon butter, melted
  • Butter or oil for cooking
  • Optional toppings: sour cream, caviar, jam, smoked salmon

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together warm milk, eggs, sugar, and salt until smooth.
  2. Gradually add flour, whisking constantly to avoid lumps.
  3. Stir in the melted butter until fully incorporated.
  4. Let the batter rest for about 10 minutes.
  5. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and lightly grease with butter or oil.
  6. Pour a small amount of batter into the skillet, tilting to spread thinly.
  7. Cook until bubbles form and edges look dry, then flip and cook the other side (about 1 minute per side).
  8. Repeat with remaining batter, greasing the skillet as needed.
  9. Serve warm with your favorite toppings!
