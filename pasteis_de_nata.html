Pastéis de Nata

Delicious Portuguese custard tarts with a crisp, flaky pastry and a creamy, caramelized custard center!

Preparation:

25 minutes

Cook:

15 minutes

Total:

40 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 sheet puff pastry, thawed
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • ⅔ cup water
  • 6 egg yolks
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Ground cinnamon (optional, for sprinkling)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 250°C (480°F). Grease a muffin tin lightly.
  2. Roll out the puff pastry slightly and cut into rounds to fit the muffin cups. Press into the tins.
  3. In a saucepan, whisk milk and flour together over medium heat until thickened. Remove from heat.
  4. In another saucepan, bring sugar and water to a boil without stirring until it reaches a syrupy consistency. Carefully pour the syrup into the milk mixture while stirring constantly.
  5. Let the mixture cool slightly, then whisk in egg yolks and vanilla until smooth.
  6. Pour the custard into the pastry-lined tins, filling about ¾ full.
  7. Bake for 12-15 minutes or until the tops are caramelized and pastry is crisp.
  8. Allow to cool slightly, then sprinkle with cinnamon if desired before serving.
