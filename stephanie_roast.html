Stephanie Roast

A hearty, comforting beef roast cooked slowly until juicy and tender, perfect for family gatherings or a cozy dinner.

Preparation:

20 minutes

Cook:

3 hours

Total:

3 hours 20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 (3 to 4 lb) beef chuck roast
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 1 cup red wine (optional)
  • 3 carrots, peeled and chopped
  • 3 potatoes, peeled and quartered
  • 2 sprigs fresh rosemary
  • 2 sprigs fresh thyme

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 160°C (325°F).
  2. Season the beef roast generously with salt and pepper on all sides.
  3. In a large oven-safe pot or Dutch oven, heat olive oil over medium-high heat.
  4. Sear the roast on all sides until browned, about 3-4 minutes per side. Remove and set aside.
  5. In the same pot, sauté onion and garlic until softened.
  6. Return the roast to the pot. Add beef broth, wine (if using), carrots, potatoes, rosemary, and thyme.
  7. Cover the pot with a lid and transfer to the oven.
  8. Cook for about 3 hours, or until the meat is fork-tender and the vegetables are soft.
  9. Let rest for 10 minutes before slicing. Serve with the vegetables and spoon the cooking juices over the top.
