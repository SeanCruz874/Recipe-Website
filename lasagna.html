Classic Lasagna

Hearty layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese — this timeless lasagna is the ultimate comfort food and a true Italian favorite perfect for gatherings and family dinners.

Preparation:

30–40 minutes active prep

Cook:

45–60 minutes

Total:

About 1 hour 30 minutes – 2 hours

Ingredients:

  • 9–12 lasagna noodles (regular or oven-ready)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 500g (1 lb) ground beef or Italian sausage
  • 2 cups marinara or tomato sauce
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil or parsley, for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). If using regular lasagna noodles, cook them according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add onion and garlic, sauté until softened.
  3. Add ground beef or sausage and cook until browned. Drain excess fat if needed.
  4. Stir in marinara sauce, tomato paste, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10–15 minutes.
  5. In a separate bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, and half of the Parmesan until well combined.
  6. In a 9x13 inch (23x33 cm) baking dish, spread a thin layer of meat sauce. Layer noodles, ricotta mixture, meat sauce, and mozzarella. Repeat layers and finish with sauce and mozzarella on top. Sprinkle remaining Parmesan.
  7. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for another 20–25 minutes, until bubbly and golden on top.
  8. Let rest for 10–15 minutes before slicing. Garnish with chopped parsley or basil, and serve warm.
