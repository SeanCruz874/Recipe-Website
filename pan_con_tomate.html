Pan con Tomate

Pan con Tomate, or "bread with tomato," is a simple yet iconic Catalan dish. With rustic bread, ripe tomatoes, olive oil, and garlic, it's perfect as a tapa, breakfast, or snack.

Preparation:

10 minutes

Cook:

5 minutes (optional, for toasting bread)

Total:

15 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 4 slices of rustic bread (such as ciabatta or sourdough)
  • 2 ripe tomatoes
  • 1 clove garlic, peeled
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt, to taste
  • Optional: sliced Jam√≥n or anchovies for topping

Instructions:

  1. Toast the bread slices until golden and crispy (you can skip this step if you prefer soft bread).
  2. Rub one side of each slice with the garlic clove while the bread is still warm.
  3. Cut the tomatoes in half. Rub the cut side over the garlic-rubbed bread to coat it with tomato pulp.
  4. Drizzle generously with olive oil and sprinkle with salt to taste.
  5. Serve immediately. Optionally, top with slices of cured ham or anchovies.
