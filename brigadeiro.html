Brigadeiro

Sweet, chocolatey, and fudgy Brazilian truffles — perfect for parties, gifts, or just treating yourself!

Preparation:

5 minutes

Cook:

10-15 minutes

Total:

20 minutes plus cooling time

Ingredients:

  • 1 can (14 oz / 395g) sweetened condensed milk
  • 2 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter (plus more for rolling)
  • Chocolate sprinkles (for coating)

Instructions:

  1. In a medium saucepan over medium heat, mix the sweetened condensed milk, cocoa powder, and butter.
  2. Keep stirring non-stop with a spatula or wooden spoon to avoid burning.
  3. Cook until the mixture thickens and you can drag your spatula across the bottom of the pan and it takes a few seconds to come back together (about 10–15 minutes).
  4. Remove from heat and let the mixture cool to room temperature.
  5. Once cool enough to handle, butter your hands, scoop small amounts of the mixture, and roll into little balls.
  6. Roll each ball in chocolate sprinkles to coat.
  7. Place in mini cupcake liners if you wanna be fancy, then serve and enjoy!
