Samgyeopsal

Samgyeopsal is a beloved Korean BBQ dish featuring thick slices of pork belly grilled at the table and enjoyed with a variety of dipping sauces and side dishes. It’s typically wrapped in lettuce with garlic, ssamjang, and kimchi for a perfect bite.

Preparation:

10–15 minutes

Cook:

15–20 minutes

Total:

25–35 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 500g (1 lb) pork belly, thickly sliced
  • Salt and black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil (for dipping)
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt (for dipping)
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper (for dipping)
  • 3–4 cloves garlic, sliced
  • 2–3 green chili peppers, sliced (optional)
  • Lettuce leaves or perilla leaves, for wrapping
  • Ssamjang (Korean dipping sauce)
  • Kimchi (for grilling or side)
  • Cooked rice, for serving

Instructions:

  1. Preheat a grill pan or tabletop grill over medium-high heat.
  2. Place the pork belly slices on the grill and cook for 3–4 minutes per side, or until golden and crispy. Grill garlic and kimchi on the side if desired.
  3. While the pork cooks, prepare the dipping sauce by mixing sesame oil with salt and pepper in a small dish.
  4. Cut cooked pork into bite-size pieces using scissors or tongs.
  5. To eat: Place a piece of pork in a lettuce or perilla leaf. Add grilled garlic, a bit of ssamjang, sliced chili, and rice or kimchi. Wrap and enjoy in one bite.
