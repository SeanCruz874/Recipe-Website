Guacamole

Guacamole is a classic Mexican avocado-based dip that’s fresh, creamy, tangy, and loaded with flavor. It’s perfect as a dip for tortilla chips, a topping for tacos, or a spread for sandwiches and burgers.

Preparation:

10 minutes

Cook:

None

Total:

10 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 3 ripe avocados
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 medium tomato, diced
  • 1–2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1–2 tablespoons lime juice (to taste)
  • 1 fresh jalapeño or serrano chile, finely chopped (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Salt, to taste
  • Freshly ground black pepper (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Cut the avocados in half, remove the pits, and scoop the flesh into a mixing bowl.
  2. Use a fork or potato masher to mash the avocados to your desired consistency—chunky or smooth.
  3. Add the onion, tomato, garlic, lime juice, chopped chile (if using), and cilantro. Mix well to combine.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Adjust lime juice or salt as needed for balance.
  5. Serve immediately with tortilla chips or as a topping. If storing, press plastic wrap directly against the surface to prevent browning and refrigerate for up to 1 day.
