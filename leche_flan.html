Leche Flan

Rich, creamy, and silky Filipino custard dessert — made with caramelized sugar and a melt-in-your-mouth texture!

Preparation:

15 minutes

Cook:

45 minutes

Total:

1 hour

Ingredients

  • 10 egg yolks
  • 1 can (14 oz) sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 can (12 oz) evaporated milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ½ cup sugar (for caramel)

Instructions

  1. In a pan over low heat, melt the sugar until golden brown. Quickly pour into molds and swirl to coat the bottom.
  2. In a large bowl, gently whisk together egg yolks, condensed milk, evaporated milk, and vanilla until smooth (do not overbeat).
  3. Strain the mixture through a fine sieve to ensure a silky texture, then pour into the caramel-coated molds.
  4. Cover with foil and steam over medium heat for 30-40 minutes or until a toothpick inserted comes out clean.
  5. Let it cool completely, then refrigerate for a few hours or overnight.
  6. To serve, run a knife around the edges and invert onto a plate.
