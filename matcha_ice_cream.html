Matcha Ice Cream

Rich, creamy, and lightly earthy â€” homemade Matcha Ice Cream is the perfect cool treat that's surprisingly easy to make.

Preparation:

20 minutes active prep

Chill/Freeze Time:

4-6 hours

Total:

About 5-7 hours

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups (360ml) heavy cream
  • 1 1/2 cups (360ml) whole milk
  • 3/4 cup (150g) sugar
  • 3 tablespoons matcha green tea powder (use good quality for best flavor)
  • 4 large egg yolks

Instructions:

  1. In a medium saucepan, warm the milk, cream, and half of the sugar over medium heat until steaming but not boiling.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk the egg yolks and the remaining sugar until pale and slightly thickened.
  3. Slowly pour some of the hot milk mixture into the egg yolks while whisking constantly to temper them.
  4. Pour the tempered yolks back into the saucepan with the rest of the milk mixture. Stir constantly over low heat until it thickens slightly and coats the back of a spoon. Do not let it boil.
  5. Remove from heat. Sift the matcha powder over the custard and whisk thoroughly until completely smooth and evenly green.
  6. Pour the mixture through a fine mesh strainer into a clean bowl to remove any lumps.
  7. Cool the mixture to room temperature, then cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, or until completely chilled.
  8. Churn the chilled custard in an ice cream maker according to the manufacturer's instructions until thick and creamy.
  9. Transfer to a container and freeze for at least 4 hours until firm enough to scoop.
